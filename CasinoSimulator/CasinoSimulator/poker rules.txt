the players may now choose to check, bet or fold based on their hand and the cards in the river.
after every player has either bet, checked or folded another card is drawn and placed in the river.
the players may now choose to check, bet or fold again based on their hand and the new cardadded to the river.
after every player has either bet, checked or folded the last card is drawn and placed in the river.
after the final bet is placed or the player has folded the players hands are revealed and the player with the best hand wins.
